# Recall — AI Study Coach

## Vision

Recall is an AI-powered study coach that transforms lecture notes into adaptive quizzes, tracks concept mastery, and generates personalized revision plans.

---

## Problem

Students often forget lecture notes after a few days.

Most AI tools summarize notes but don't help students remember them.

Students don't know:

- what to revise
- what they're weak at
- when to revise

---

## Solution

Recall uses AI to

- understand notes
- extract concepts
- generate adaptive quizzes
- detect weak concepts
- build personalized revision plans

---

## Target Users

- College Students
- Online Learners
- Interview Preparation
- Competitive Exam Aspirants

---

## Core Features

- Paste Notes
- AI Concept Extraction
- Knowledge Map
- Adaptive Quiz
- AI Tutor
- Revision Planner
- Dashboard

---
